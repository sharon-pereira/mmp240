City College of New York

Electronic Design and Multimedia

Degree: B.F.A

This section is a professional program leading to the B.F.A. degree. The 75 credit major culminates in a semester-long Thesis Project. Application to the B.F.A. is by portfolio. Courses include: Graphic Design Imaging Illustration Multimedia 3D Design Web Design Typography Animation Video Interactive Design Digital Photography
